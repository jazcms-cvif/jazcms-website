Mission & Vision

Guided by science, driven by a commitment to the conservation of Philippine marine ecosystems.

🎯

Our Mission

To conduct rigorous, community-relevant marine science research that generates knowledge supporting the conservation, management, and sustainable use of Bohol's coastal and marine resources. We are committed to training the next generation of Filipino marine scientists and engaging local communities in stewardship of their seas.

🔭

Our Vision

To be a leading regional marine research institution in the Philippines, recognized for excellence in biodiversity science, molecular ecology, and evidence-based coastal management. We envision a future where the rich marine heritage of Bohol is understood, valued, and protected for generations to come.

Research Focus Areas

Our interdisciplinary research spans four core domains of marine science.

🪸

Biodiversity

Systematic species surveys of coral reefs, seagrass beds, and intertidal zones in Bohol.

🐚

Marine Molluscs

Taxonomy, distribution, and ecology of gastropods, bivalves, and cephalopods in Central Visayas.

🧬

DNA Extraction & Sequencing

Molecular barcoding and phylogenetic analysis of marine organisms using COI and other gene markers.

💧

Water Quality

Monitoring physicochemical parameters and assessing their impact on reef health and biodiversity.
